Program Principles

🌅

Morning = Activation

Mobility, stretching, and nervous system work happen before school. Cortisol is naturally high — use it. This sets the body up for a productive practice.

🏀

Pre-Practice = Priming

Short plyometric and activation blocks (10–15 min) right before practice increase fast-twitch fiber recruitment. Science shows this elevates performance for 30–90 min.

🌙

Evening = Recovery

Foam rolling and myofascial work are more effective after the body has been working all day. Post-practice rolling reduces DOMS and speeds tissue repair.

💤

Rest Days Are Built In

Sun + Wed are intentional off days. Adaptation happens during rest, not training. ADHD brains especially benefit from scheduled decompression.

🧠

Structure Over Willpower

This app replaces decision fatigue with a clear checklist. You don't need to remember what to do — just open the app and follow the day.
